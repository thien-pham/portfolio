PROJECTS

Recipedia

Ever had that one restaurant dish that you're just itching to recreate, maybe with a few personal tweaks? Welcome to Recipedia, the app for the foodie on a budget. Get inspired to dine at your favorite restaurant at home. Recipedia integrates its own database with the Yelp API to search for restaurants and return user-submitted recipes inspired by them.
